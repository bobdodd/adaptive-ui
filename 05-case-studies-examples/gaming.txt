Title:
Adapting classic games to the Sonic design space: Tetris as a case study
Brief description:
How do you take classic games like Tetris, and make them usable by blind and visually impaired users? Without any loss of features, and without distorting the game? A look at the design techniques and processes necessary to achieve this.
Longer description:
As designers, we work in three design spaces: visual, sonic, and haptic. Each of those spaces has its own characteristics in terms of information bandwidth
